Learn Pattern Logic

Nested Loops

Patterns are deeply reliant on nested loops. The outer loop dictates rows (adding newlines), while the inner loops handle the columns (printing the spaces and characters).

Rows & Columns

Think of patterns like a grid or matrix. To navigate it, your outer for loop iterates through rows (Y-axis), and your inner for loop iterates through the X-axis.

Common Tricks

Using blank spaces correctly is key to making triangles and diamonds. Learn to manage spaces in a separate inner loop before printing symbols to create shifting shapes.
